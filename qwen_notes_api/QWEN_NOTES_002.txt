6. **Channel Lifecycle:**

7. **Listener Loop Pattern:**
   - Incoming messages are handled in background daemon threads.
   - Exceptions in handlers are caught to prevent subsequent handlers from being affected.

8. **Event Flow:**
   - Channel events (`CHANNEL_MESSAGE_RECEIVED` and `CHANNEL_MESSAGE_SENT`) are published to the `EventBus`.
   - These events can be subscribed to by other modules for additional processing or logging.
